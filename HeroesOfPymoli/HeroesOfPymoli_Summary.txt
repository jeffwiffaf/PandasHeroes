1. The profit is mainly driven by male players in this game because majority of the players are male. 
However, female players are willing to spend more on average on in-game purchase item than male players.

the users in the game.  They are also one of the highest age group in purchase per person. 

3. Item ID 178 is the most purchase item in the game and it's also the most profitable item.  Also, there
